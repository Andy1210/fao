Fake Artist Online

An online multiplayer aid for Oink Games' tabletop game, A Fake Artist Goes to New York.

Rules

A group of artists must work together to find the one fake artist among them, while that person tries to avoid detection.

Each round, all artists receive a random keyword and its hint/category, e.g. "Apple (Food)".

But the faker only receives the hint.

Players take turns drawing single strokes on the drawing pad. After every player has drawn twice, everyone votes on who they think the fake artist is.

The fake artist has to bluff their way through the drawing, while the artists try to draw enough to prove they know the keyword - but draw too obviously, and the fake artist might catch on!

And even if the fake artist gets caught at the end, if they're able to correctly guess the keyword, they still win!

About

This is an unofficial hobby project, and a continual WIP. You might encounter bugs.

Please use a modern browser!

There's no voting or chat system right now.
